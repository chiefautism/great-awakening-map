## 1. Draco Reptilians

The Draco Reptilian conspiracy theory is one of the most elaborate and far-reaching claims in the UFO and conspiracy communities, primarily popularized by British author and speaker David Icke in his 1999 book *The Biggest Secret*. According to Icke, tall, blood-drinking, shape-shifting reptilian humanoids originating from the [Alpha Draconis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reptilian_conspiracy_theory) star system have secretly controlled human civilization for thousands of years by infiltrating positions of political, financial, and royal power. Icke contends that these reptilians interbred with select human bloodlines millennia ago to create a hybrid race he calls the "Babylonian Brotherhood," whose modern descendants include the British Royal Family, the [Rockefellers, Rothschilds](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Icke), and allegedly all 43 (as of 2003) American presidents. The theory holds that these hybrids can shapeshift between human and reptilian form, but must consume human blood or participate in blood rituals to maintain their human disguise, which Icke ties to allegations of ritual sacrifice among global elites. Icke further claims these beings originate not only from another star system but from the "lower fourth dimension" or "lower astral dimension," and that [Archon entities](https://discover.hubpages.com/politics/David-Icke-and-the-mystery-of-the-Reptilians) feed on negative human emotional energy — fear, hatred, and suffering — which is why they engineer perpetual wars and social chaos. Critics have extensively noted the theory's [antisemitic overtones](https://content.time.com/time/specials/packages/article/0,28804,1860871_1860876_1861029,00.html), particularly in Icke's identification of Jewish banking families as reptilians and his endorsement of *The Protocols of the Elders of Zion*, though Icke himself denies any antisemitic intent.

Within the broader Draco lore, a detailed hierarchy has been constructed by various conspiracy authors and self-described insiders. At the top sit the [Ciakar](https://thinkaboutit-aliens.com/think-aboutits-alien-type-summary-the-ciakar/), described as the royal caste of Draco Reptilians — enormous winged beings standing 14 to 22 feet tall, weighing up to 1,800 pounds, with dark green or brown impenetrable scales, yellowish reptilian eyes, multiple spinal columns, and allegedly two hearts. Below the Ciakar royals are the Draco Warriors, a [military caste](https://livingsignalresearch.com/the-reptilians) standing 7 to 8 feet tall, feared across the galaxy for their fighting prowess, and frequently cited in claims about Deep Underground Military Base (DUMB) conflicts. According to alleged Dulce Base security officer Thomas Castello, the [command hierarchy](https://www.truthcontrol.com/forum/draconians-and-earth-based-reptilians) flows from Alpha Draconis Dracos down to Earth-based Reptilians, then to Tall Greys, and finally to small Greys — a cascading chain of extraterrestrial authority operating beneath the surface of our planet.

The Dulce Base narrative represents one of the most dramatic claims in Draco Reptilian lore. Phil Schneider, an explosive engineer who claimed high-level government security clearance, alleged that in August 1979 he participated in drilling operations for a secret underground facility near [Dulce, New Mexico](https://strangestrangestrange.com/paranormal/ufo/phil-schneider-dulce-base/), when workers breached a cavern housing alien beings. Schneider stated he came face-to-face with a 7-foot-tall grey alien, immediately drew his pistol and killed two of them, but a third alien fired a chest-mounted weapon that [blew off several of his fingers](https://www.huffpost.com/entry/that-time-subterranean-al_b_5182945) and opened his chest cavity. He claimed the encounter escalated into a full-scale firefight that killed approximately 60 U.S. military personnel, including Green Berets. Schneider spent years on the lecture circuit displaying his missing fingers as evidence, and claimed the U.S. government maintained over 1,400 similar underground bases worldwide, 192 within America alone, all interconnected by [high-speed tunnel systems](https://allthatsinteresting.com/dulce-base). Schneider was [found dead](https://sofrep.com/news/the-legend-of-green-berets-fighting-aliens-beneath-dulce-new-mexico/) in his apartment in January 1996 with a piano wire wrapped around his neck, his presentation materials and research having vanished — a death his supporters claim was assassination to silence him, while skeptics note there exists zero physical evidence of any firefight, no shell casings, no official records of 60 soldiers dying in New Mexico that year, and no corroborating witnesses.

## 2. Greys (Zeta Reticulans)

The Grey aliens are arguably the most recognizable alleged extraterrestrial species in popular culture, described as small humanoid beings with smooth grey skin, disproportionately large hairless heads, and large black almond-shaped eyes. Within UFO conspiracy communities, they are commonly referred to as [Zeta Reticulans](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grey_alien), a name derived from the Zeta Reticuli binary star system approximately 39 light-years from Earth. This stellar association traces directly to the 1961 abduction case of [Barney and Betty Hill](https://hangar1publishing.com/blogs/ufos-uaps-and-aliens/zeta-reticuli), the first widely publicized alien abduction claim in modern history. Under hypnosis, Betty Hill described being shown a star map by her captors; in 1969, schoolteacher Marjorie Fish constructed a three-dimensional model of nearby stars and concluded the aliens' home system matched Zeta Reticuli — a claim that became foundational to Grey alien mythology despite significant astronomical criticism. UFO lore distinguishes between at least two types of Greys: the [small Greys](https://university.awakenche.org/knowledge-base/zeta-reticulans-zetas-greys-various-types/), standing 3 to 4 feet tall, described as drone-like workers operating under a hive mind or swarm intelligence, and the Tall Greys, standing 5 to 7 feet tall, who serve as leaders, scientists, and diplomats within the Grey hierarchy. The smaller Greys are typically described as sexless beings who reproduce exclusively through cloning technology, having allegedly lost the ability to reproduce biologically due to millennia of genetic degradation.

The Roswell incident of July 1947 is central to Grey alien lore, though the story evolved significantly over decades. The initial event involved the recovery of debris from a [ranch near Roswell, New Mexico](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roswell_incident), which the U.S. Army Air Forces initially announced as a "flying disc" before retracting the statement and identifying the debris as a weather balloon. Decades later, the Air Force revealed the debris was from [Project Mogul](https://www.britannica.com/event/Roswell-incident), a classified program using high-altitude balloons to monitor Soviet nuclear tests. Critically, none of the original 1947 reports mentioned alien bodies; the claim of recovered extraterrestrial corpses [only emerged later](https://www.skyatnightmagazine.com/space-science/roswell-ufo-incident) in the 1970s and 1980s as researchers re-interviewed aging witnesses. A 1995 "alien autopsy" film purporting to show examination of a Roswell body was [confirmed as a hoax in 2006](https://science.howstuffworks.com/space/aliens-ufos/history-roswell-incident.htm) by its creator, though he maintained it was a "reconstruction" of lost genuine footage. The Air Force's 1997 report suggested that stories of alien bodies likely originated from witnesses conflating memories of parachute crash test dummies, an injured airman, and charred bodies from an unrelated plane crash.

Perhaps the most consequential Grey conspiracy theory involves the alleged [Greada Treaty](https://vocal.media/futurism/the-greada-treaty) of February 20, 1954, in which President Dwight Eisenhower supposedly met Grey aliens at Edwards (then Muroc) Air Force Base and negotiated a formal agreement. According to former naval intelligence officer William Cooper and other claimants, the treaty permitted the Greys to [abduct a limited number of humans](https://alessandragemmo.com/secret-treaties-with-aliens-myth-or-missing-history/) for medical and genetic experimentation, on the condition that subjects would be returned unharmed with their memories erased. In exchange, the U.S. government received advanced technology later reverse-engineered in black-budget programs, allegedly yielding stealth aircraft, fiber optics, and night vision technology. The narrative holds that the Greys subsequently [violated the treaty](https://www.exopaedia.org/Greada+Treaty), conducting far more abductions than agreed and performing invasive genetic experiments, including hybrid breeding programs designed to create human-Grey hybrids. Conspiracy theorists claim this led to underground skirmishes between U.S. military forces and Grey aliens. However, extensive Freedom of Information Act searches, declassified presidential records, [National Archives holdings](https://pandemictimeline.com/1954/02/pres-eisenhower-enters-into-the-greada-treaty/), and Eisenhower Presidential Library documents contain absolutely no authenticated treaty, memorandum, or contemporaneous correspondence supporting any such meeting or agreement.

## 3. Nordics / Tall Whites

The Nordic aliens are among the oldest alleged extraterrestrial types in modern UFO lore, tracing back to the contactee movement of the 1950s. George Adamski, a Polish-American author and self-proclaimed contactee, claimed that on November 20, 1952, in the [California desert near Desert Center](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_Adamski), he met a being named Orthon who arrived in a Venusian "scout ship." Adamski described Orthon and other aliens as [beautiful, human-like beings](https://science.howstuffworks.com/space/aliens-ufos/george-adamski.htm) with light skin, flowing hair, and gentle demeanors — the prototype for what would become known as "Nordic aliens" or "Space Brothers." Adamski claimed these aliens hailed from Venus, Mars, and Saturn, and he authored three books about his experiences: *Flying Saucers Have Landed* (1953, co-written with Desmond Leslie), *Inside the Space Ships* (1955), and *Flying Saucers Farewell* (1961). He produced photographs he claimed showed alien [scout craft photographed through his 6-inch telescope](https://www.history.com/articles/george-adamski-ufo-alien-photos), though most UFO researchers and investigators ultimately regarded him as [a charlatan and con artist](https://web2.ph.utexas.edu/~coker2/index.files/sbrothers.shtml), concluding his photographs showed small models. Despite this, Adamski drew thousands of followers and essentially created the template for benevolent humanoid extraterrestrials — tall, beautiful, Scandinavian-looking beings concerned with nuclear proliferation and humanity's spiritual development — that persists in UFO culture to this day.

The Tall Whites represent a more recent and distinct Nordic-type claim, primarily based on the testimony of Charles James Hall, born in 1944, who holds a master's degree in nuclear physics and served as a U.S. Air Force weather observer. Hall claims that during his [two-year assignment at Nellis Air Force Base](https://www.bibliotecapleyades.net/vida_alien/esp_hall02.htm) (specifically the Indian Springs Gunnery Range, roughly 50 miles northwest of Las Vegas) from 1965 to 1967, he had extensive encounters with a race of extraterrestrials he calls the "Tall Whites." He describes them as humanoid beings with [chalk-white skin](https://medium.com/@noahhradek/evidence-for-the-testimony-of-charles-hall-and-the-tall-whites-3e8a38cfae41), large blue eyes that wrap partway around their heads, small noses, tiny ears pressed tight to their scalps, and thin straight blonde hair. They range from 6 to over 8 feet tall (growing taller with age), can allegedly run at 40 miles per hour, and live approximately 800 years. Their speech resembles a dog barking or bird chirping, though they have learned English and use helmet-like devices to project emotions and communicate telepathically. Hall's first sighting occurred in June 1965 near a weather shack; a subsequent encounter involved a [juvenile Tall White whose stun device misfired](https://www.tvi.show/nexus/the-tall-whites-charles-halls-alien-encounters-at-nellis-air-force-base), searing Hall's thigh with a blue-white beam that left a scar he claims was verified by a 2010 private biopsy showing anomalous plasma burns. Hall describes their spacecraft as white, tic-tac-shaped, approximately 60 feet long, silent at speeds up to 8,000 mph, and capable of defying sonic booms through unknown technology. He claims they originate from [a planet near Arcturus](https://oddwoo.com/charles-hall-tall-whites.php), 36 light-years away, and have maintained a Nevada base for centuries under a secret U.S. government agreement allegedly tracing to 1954 Holloman AFB meetings. Hall published his accounts beginning in 2002 in the *Millennial Hospitality* book series (six volumes by 2013) and gained further attention through [Coast to Coast AM](https://www.fringearchives.com/charles-hall-and-the-tall-whites-inside-an-alien-encounter/) appearances and a 2020 docudrama, *Walking with the Tall Whites*.

## 4. Pleiadians

The Pleiadian contactee movement is one of the most prominent and controversial threads in UFO culture, and its central figure is Eduard Albert "Billy" Meier, a Swiss farmer born in 1937 who claims extraterrestrial contact spanning his entire life. Meier alleges his encounters began in 1942 at age five, when he met an elderly Plejaren man named Sfath, and resumed on [January 28, 1975](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Billy_Meier), when he met Semjase, described as the granddaughter of Sfath and a female Plejaren from the planet Erra in the Pleiades star cluster. Meier produced hundreds of photographs and several films purporting to show Plejaren "beamships" floating above the Swiss countryside, along with metal samples, sound recordings, and detailed written contact notes running to thousands of pages. His claims generated a devoted following and the formation of FIGU (Free Community of Interests for Fringe and Spiritual Sciences and Ufological Studies) in Switzerland. However, the photographic evidence faced devastating challenges: in 1997, [Meier's ex-wife Kalliope](https://uforeport.com/the-billy-meier-contactee-case/) told interviewers that his UFO photos were of models crafted from household objects, including garbage can lids and other common materials. Furthermore, photographs Meier presented as depicting extraterrestrial women named "Asket" and "Nera" were [conclusively identified](https://www.futureofmankind.co.uk/Billy_Meier/Asket_and_Nera_Photos) as images of Michelle DellaFave and Susan Lund, members of the American dance troupe The Golddiggers, filmed during *The Dean Martin Show*. Author Kal K. Korff conducted extensive fieldwork at Meier's property and published [*Spaceships of the Pleiades*](https://books.google.com/books/about/Spaceships_of_the_Pleiades.html?id=SIIgAQAAIAAJ), documenting evidence that Meier constructed his UFOs from garbage can lids, wedding cake decorations, and model parts, while noting that all original photographic negatives had either been "lost in the mail" or "stolen" — making scientific analysis impossible.

Beyond Meier, the Pleiadian channeling movement expanded dramatically through the work of Barbara Marciniak, an internationally known trance channel from North Carolina. Her 1992 book [*Bringers of the Dawn: Teachings from the Pleiadians*](https://www.amazon.com/Bringers-Dawn-Pleiadians-Barbara-Marciniak/dp/093968098X) was compiled from more than 400 hours of channeled sessions and became a foundational text of New Age Pleiadian spirituality. Marciniak claims to trance-channel a collective of multidimensional beings from the Pleiades who impart wisdom about human evolution, DNA activation, and cosmic history. The channeled Pleiadians in her work describe the [original human as a magnificent being](https://www.pleiadians.com/dawn.html) with twelve strands of DNA and twelve chakra centers (compared to the two active DNA strands and seven chakras in current understanding), claiming that humanity was deliberately "downgraded" by malevolent entities. Her books — including *Earth*, *Family of Light*, and *Path of Empowerment* — have collectively been [translated into more than twenty languages](https://www.simonandschuster.com/books/Bringers-of-the-Dawn/Barbara-Marciniak/9780939680986) and sold over 500,000 copies. Marciniak's Pleiadians advise becoming "media-free," working in teams, eliminating the words "should" and "try" from one's vocabulary, and learning to go beyond fear. The broader Pleiadian channeling movement has spawned numerous additional channels, books, and online communities, making the Pleiadians one of the most commercially successful and culturally pervasive alleged alien races in the New Age spiritual marketplace.

## 5. Arcturians

The Arcturians represent one of the most benevolent alleged extraterrestrial civilizations in New Age cosmology, and their origin in alternative spirituality traces to the American mystic Edgar Cayce (1877-1945), widely known as "The Sleeping Prophet." Cayce, who delivered over 14,000 psychic readings during his lifetime, mentioned the star Arcturus in [more than 30 readings](https://in5d.com/edgar-cayce-talking-about-the-arcturians/), describing it as a gateway to higher realms of consciousness. In Reading 2828-1 (1932), Cayce declared that [Arcturus is "the highest civilization in our galaxy"](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arcturians_(New_Age)) — beings who have mastered both technology and spirituality, using light and sound for healing and interstellar travel. He characterized Arcturian souls as having a profound effect on human development, stating that the star served as a kind of waystation or transition point for souls moving between lifetimes, and described the Arcturians as [fifth-dimensional life-forms](http://fixedstarsinfo.blogspot.com/2016/04/edgar-cayce-on-arcturians.html) who had transcended the physical limitations that constrain humanity. While Cayce did not describe Arcturians in the detailed physical terms that later authors would adopt, his readings established the foundational association between Arcturus and advanced spiritual civilization that subsequent channelers would elaborate upon for decades.

The modern Arcturian movement took shape primarily through two key figures. Norma Milanovich's 1990 book [*We, the Arcturians*](https://grokipedia.com/page/Arcturians_(New_Age)) is widely regarded as the foundational modern text that introduced Arcturians to a broad audience, describing them as benevolent, high-dimensional planetary healers who communicate telepathically with receptive humans. Milanovich portrayed the Arcturians as tall, slender beings with large almond-shaped eyes, operating from massive starships and devoted to guiding Earth through a period of spiritual transformation. David K. Miller further developed the Arcturian narrative as one of the [most influential figures](https://hero.fandom.com/wiki/Arcturians) in the modern channeling movement, founding the Group of Forty — a global network of meditation circles — and claiming direct telepathic contact with Arcturian beings since the 1990s. In the broader New Age framework, Arcturians are described as masters of [sacred geometry, quantum healing, and frequency-based systems](https://www.galacticfederationoflight.com/blogs/starseed/arcturian-technology), transmitting "light codes" — geometric symbols or numerical sequences appearing during dreams and visions — that activate dormant DNA and elevate human consciousness. The Arcturian starseed community has become one of the largest in the New Age movement, with adherents identifying themselves as souls originating from Arcturus who incarnated on Earth to assist in [humanity's ascension](https://www.centreofexcellence.com/arcturian-starseeds/) from third-dimensional to fifth-dimensional reality. Characteristic traits attributed to Arcturian starseeds include strong empathy, natural healing abilities, fascination with technology, and a deep sense of cosmic purpose, though all of these concepts derive entirely from channeled spiritual teachings rather than any scientific evidence.

## 6. Sirians

The Sirian extraterrestrial hypothesis centers on the claim that beings from the Sirius star system — the brightest star in Earth's night sky, located approximately 8.6 light-years away — played a direct role in the development of ancient human civilizations, particularly Egypt and West Africa. The most academically influential presentation of this claim came from Robert K.G. Temple's 1976 book [*The Sirius Mystery*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Sirius_Mystery), published by St. Martin's Press, which argued that the Dogon people of Mali in West Africa preserve traditions of contact with intelligent extraterrestrial beings from the Sirius system. Temple's evidence derived primarily from the fieldwork of French anthropologists Marcel Griaule and Germaine Dieterlen, who in the 1930s and 1940s documented [Dogon mythological traditions](https://www.gaia.com/article/did-this-african-tribe-originate-in-another-star-system) containing surprisingly detailed astronomical knowledge. The Dogon allegedly knew about Sirius B — a white dwarf companion star invisible to the naked eye and not photographed by Western astronomers until 1970 — and accurately described its [50-year elliptical orbit](https://hangar1publishing.com/blogs/ufos-uaps-and-aliens/the-dogon-tribe-and-sirius) around Sirius A. Temple hypothesized that amphibious extraterrestrial beings called the Nommo visited the Dogon's ancestors and taught them advanced astronomical knowledge, and further argued that these same beings originated the systems of the Pharaohs of Egypt, Greek mythology, and the [Epic of Gilgamesh](https://www.amazon.com/Sirius-Mystery-Scientific-Evidence-Contact/dp/089281750X).

The connection between Sirius and ancient Egypt runs deep in both mainstream archaeology and alternative theories. The ancient Egyptians worshipped Sirius as the goddess [Sopdet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sopdet), guarantor of the fertility of their land, whose heliacal rising (first appearance before dawn after a period of invisibility) marked the beginning of the Egyptian calendar year and predicted the annual [Nile flood](https://wilderness-ventures-egypt.com/sirius-rising-and-the-ancient-egyptian-calendar/). Sopdet was later syncretized with the goddess Isis, while the constellation Orion (Sah) was associated with Osiris, creating a celestial mythology in which the [brightest star and most recognizable constellation](https://www.gaia.com/article/star-sirius-and-egypt) mirrored the most important divine couple in the Egyptian pantheon. Alternative theorists in the New Age community extend this to claim that actual Sirian extraterrestrials were the architects of Egyptian civilization, allegedly helping build the Pyramids and temples, providing [advanced astronomical and medical knowledge](https://www.debbiesolaris.com/sirius), and also influencing the Maya. However, the empirical foundations of Temple's Sirius Mystery have been severely challenged: in 1991, Belgian anthropologist Walter van Beek led a [return expedition](https://www.ancient-origins.net/news-mysterious-phenomena/did-ancient-aliens-impart-advanced-astronomical-knowledge-dogon-tribe-021403) to the Dogon and found no trace of the detailed Sirius lore reported by Griaule. Journalist James Oberg further argued that the Dogon's astronomical knowledge [resembled European speculations of the late 1920s](https://chandra.harvard.edu/chronicle/0400/sirius_part2.html), suggesting cultural contamination from missionaries, traders, or French colonial administrators rather than ancient extraterrestrial contact. The distinction between Sirius A (a main-sequence star twice the mass of our Sun) and Sirius B (a dense, faint white dwarf) remains central to the debate — the question being whether the Dogon could have independently known of Sirius B before its Western scientific confirmation.

## 7. Anunnaki

Zecharia Sitchin (1920-2010), a Russian-born American author who claimed reading ability in Sumerian cuneiform, constructed what is arguably the most elaborate ancient astronaut narrative ever published. Beginning with [*The 12th Planet*](https://medium.com/@chapati2323/summary-of-the-12th-planet-by-zecharia-sitchin-8058baa66079) in 1976 and continuing through a dozen subsequent books collectively known as *The Earth Chronicles*, Sitchin proposed that the Anunnaki — a group of deities in ancient Sumerian, Akkadian, and Babylonian mythology — were in fact advanced extraterrestrial beings from a planet called Nibiru. According to Sitchin, Nibiru occupies a highly elongated orbit that brings it into the inner solar system [every 3,600 years](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zecharia_Sitchin), and when the Anunnaki first arrived on Earth approximately 450,000 years ago, they established operations in Mesopotamia (modern Iraq) to mine gold — a mineral they desperately needed to repair their planet's deteriorating atmosphere. Sitchin identified key Anunnaki figures from Sumerian texts: Anu, the supreme ruler who remained on Nibiru; [Enki](https://www.annunaki.org/enki-enlil/), the brilliant scientist and chief of operations on Earth who sympathized with humanity; and Enlil, the stern administrator and Enki's half-brother who enforced divine hierarchy and viewed humans as expendable labor. When the Anunnaki rank-and-file workers rebelled against the grueling mining work, Enki and the chief medical officer Ninhursag allegedly used [genetic manipulation and in vitro fertilization](https://www.historyhidden.org/the-anunnaki-and-ancient-astronauts/) to splice Anunnaki DNA with that of Homo erectus, creating the first Homo sapiens — "primitive workers" — to replace the rebellious Anunnaki miners in African gold operations.

Sitchin wove his Anunnaki narrative deeply into known Mesopotamian literature, interpreting the Sumerian creation poem *Enuma Elish* as a coded account of planetary formation and collision, and connecting the Anunnaki story to the [Epic of Gilgamesh](https://gofinkler.com/anunnaki-myth-history/) through the flood narrative. In the standard Akkadian version of Gilgamesh, the god Ea (the Akkadian equivalent of Enki) warns Utnapishtim of an impending flood sent by the gods and instructs him to build a vessel — a story with obvious parallels to the biblical Noah. Sitchin interpreted this as a factual account: Enlil had decreed humanity's destruction by flood (which Sitchin associated with the gravitational effects of Nibiru's periodic approach), while Enki defied his brother to save a remnant of the human species he had helped create. The conflict between [Enlil and Enki](https://www.scribd.com/document/480460241/Enki-Enlil-Annunaki-org) — authoritarian enforcer versus compassionate creator — became the central dramatic axis of Sitchin's entire body of work, with Sitchin arguing it underlies not just Sumerian mythology but the entire tradition of dualistic theology (God vs. Satan, order vs. chaos) in Western civilization. Sitchin's books sold [millions of copies worldwide](https://medium.com/@stphngeist/the-fascinating-story-of-the-anunnaki-part-1-2c8e1eed71f) and were translated into more than 25 languages.

However, Sitchin's work has been comprehensively dismantled by scholars of ancient Near Eastern languages, most notably by Michael S. Heiser (1963-2023), a scholar of biblical languages who held a Ph.D. in Hebrew Bible and Semitic Languages from the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Heiser created and maintained [SitchinIsWrong.com](https://www.sitchiniswrong.com/), a detailed website demonstrating that Sitchin's translations and interpretations consistently contradict the actual content of cuneiform tablets as understood by trained Assyriologists. Heiser's central point was that the ancient Mesopotamians compiled [their own dictionaries](https://sitchiniswrong.com/anunnaki/anunnaki.htm) — published since the mid-twentieth century — and the words Sitchin claimed referred to rocket ships, space travel, and genetic engineering have no such meanings according to the Mesopotamians themselves. Heiser publicly challenged Sitchin to produce [even one line of cuneiform text](https://sitchiniswrong.com/open_letter.htm) demonstrating that the Anunnaki inhabit a planet called Nibiru, or that "Anunnaki" means "people of the fiery rockets" — a challenge Sitchin never accepted. Heiser demonstrated that "Nibiru" in actual Sumerian and Akkadian usage means "place of crossing" or "crossing fee" (as seen in the [Gilgamesh epic](https://sitchiniswrong.com/nibirunew.pdf)), not a twelfth planet. No modern astronomical survey has ever detected a large planet on a 3,600-year orbit, and Sitchin was not, as he implied, a trained scholar of ancient languages — a fact Heiser emphasized in his [academic critiques](https://drmsh.com/exposing-the-work-of-zecharia-sitchin-part-2-does-the-bible-have-the-gods-creating-humankind/).

## 8. Mantids / Insectoids

Mantis beings — also called Mantids or Insectoids — occupy a peculiar and increasingly prominent position in both UFO abduction research and psychedelic science, representing one of the few entity types that appear with striking consistency across completely unrelated experiential domains. In abduction literature, researchers such as Dr. David Jacobs of Temple University and the late [Dr. John E. Mack](https://www.uapdigest.com/woo-wednesday-mantis-beings-insectoid-aliens/) of Harvard Medical School catalogued hundreds of accounts describing towering mantis-like aliens, typically 6 to 9 feet tall, with triangular heads, enormous compound eyes, and long segmented limbs, who oversee medical and genetic procedures performed by smaller Grey entities. The consistent detail across abductee testimony is one of hierarchy: [mantis beings are described as supervisors](https://politicalsaucer.substack.com/p/the-mantis-phenomenon-how-identical) or directors who stand behind or above the Greys during examinations, sometimes wearing robes of various colors — with purple reportedly indicating the highest rank. Abductees frequently describe the mantis entities as exuding a calm, almost clinical authority, sometimes communicating telepathically and apparently capable of controlling human emotional states, which they allegedly "harvest" or feed upon. The recurring description of mantis beings as occupying a [command position over Grey aliens](https://nexusnewsfeed.com/article/consciousness/the-mantis-phenomenon-how-identical-entities-appear-across-psychedelic-experiences-ufo-encounters-and-ancient-mythology/) has led some UFO researchers to propose a multi-species hierarchy in which insectoids are the true intelligence behind abduction programs.

The mantis phenomenon gained significant scientific attention through psychedelic research, particularly studies involving N,N-dimethyltryptamine (DMT). A landmark 2020 study published in the *Journal of Psychopharmacology* by researchers at Johns Hopkins University surveyed [over 2,500 people](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/full/10.1177/0269881120916143) about their most memorable DMT entity encounter. While insectoid or praying mantis entities comprised approximately 2.3% of reported encounters (40 cases), their appearance is notable for its extreme specificity and consistency across unrelated subjects. Broader analyses of DMT entity types show that approximately [9% of entity reports involve creatures](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC8716686/) — reptilian, tentacled, insectoid, and feline presences — with mantis beings forming a distinct and recognizable subcategory. The Johns Hopkins study found that 78% of respondents encountered "benevolent" entities, 70% described them as "sacred," and 81% said [the encounter felt "more real"](https://www.iflscience.com/people-seeing-strange-entities-when-taking-dmt-could-be-the-source-of-alien-abductions-55980) than anything they had previously experienced. Some researchers, including psychologist Rick Strassman, have proposed that the overlap between DMT entity encounters and alien abduction accounts suggests either a common neurological mechanism or — more speculatively — that DMT grants access to the same "dimension" that abductees inadvertently enter. The convergence of [identical mantis descriptions](https://www.theinteldrop.org/2025/08/03/the-mantis-phenomenon-how-identical-entities-appear-across-psychedelic-experiences-ufo-encounters-and-ancient-mythology/) across psychedelic experiences, UFO abductions, and even ancient mythological traditions (praying mantis figures appear in San rock art and other indigenous traditions) has become one of the most genuinely puzzling patterns in this field, defying easy dismissal even by skeptics who note the lack of physical evidence.

## 9. Andromedans

The Andromedan contactee narrative is built almost entirely upon the claims of a single individual: Alex Collier (a pseudonym), an American who asserts lifetime contact with extraterrestrial beings from the [Andromeda constellation](https://www.alexcollier.org/) — not the Andromeda galaxy, though the distinction is often blurred in popular accounts. Collier claims his first contact occurred in 1964 in the upper peninsula of Michigan during childhood, and that his primary contacts are two beings named [Morenae and Vissaeus](https://archive.org/details/defending-sacred-ground-the-andromedan-compendium-1997-alex-collier), members of a race called the Zenetaeans. He describes Morenae as having light blue skin, while Vissaeus, described as much older, has whitish skin having lost most of the blue pigmentation with age. According to Collier, the Andromedans are a fifth-density telepathic race who can read and feel each other's emotions, live to an average age of 2,007 years, and exist on a dimensional level above current human consciousness. They communicate primarily through telepathy and holographic imagery, and their civilization allegedly operates without money, class hierarchy, or organized religion.

Collier's most comprehensive presentation of his claims came in his 1996 publication [*Defending Sacred Ground: The Andromedan Compendium*](https://www.alexcollier.org/alex-collier-defending-sacred-ground-1996/), which he distributed freely. In this work, based on information allegedly transmitted by Morenae and Vissaeus, Collier outlines an ambitious alternative history of the galaxy. He claims that all human races in the galaxy [originate from the Lyra constellation](https://www.alexcollier.org/alex-collier-galactic-history-according-to-alex-collier-and-the-andromedans/), that the first genetic melding between a human species and a primate race occurred 28,731,007 B.C., and that Native American races are remnants of ancient Babylonians brought to the Americas by the Sumerian god Enki. The Andromedans are described as part of a [council that monitors galactic events](https://mystfiles.com/andromedans-guardians-of-the-cosmic-balance-from-the-andromeda-galaxy/) to prevent large-scale conflicts, and they allegedly warned Collier about a regressive extraterrestrial agenda to enslave humanity through manipulation of governments and the media. Collier gained visibility through a 1994 television interview and subsequent conference appearances, and his website continues to archive his lectures and writings. However, from a scientific standpoint, the Andromedans' existence remains entirely unverified — the vast distances involved, the lack of any physical evidence, and the exclusively anecdotal nature of Collier's accounts make the claims [speculative at best](https://mystfiles.com/andromedans-guardians-of-the-cosmic-balance-from-the-andromeda-galaxy/). Collier has faced criticism for his use of a pseudonym, the untestable nature of his claims, and parallels between his narratives and pre-existing science fiction and New Age literature.

## 10. Lyrans

The Lyrans hold a foundational position in New Age galactic mythology as the alleged original humanoid race in the Milky Way galaxy — the progenitors from whom virtually all other human and human-like extraterrestrial civilizations descended. According to channeled accounts, primarily from authors such as Lyssa Royal Holt (author of *The Prism of Lyra*) and information attributed to the Billy Meier Pleiadian contacts, the Lyra constellation hosted the [first advanced humanoid civilization](https://www.truthcontrol.com/pictures/lyrans-and-vegans-our-cosmic-human-ancestors-who-are-ancestral-line-all-human-races-our-gal) in our galaxy, with the star Vega serving as its central hub. The Lyrans are portrayed as having achieved extremely high [technological and spiritual development](https://dragonattheendoftime.com/lyrans-founders-of-form-in-the-one-god-universe/) over millions of years, mastering interstellar travel and establishing colonies in numerous star systems, making them the "founding fathers" of the galactic humanoid family.

The central catastrophe in Lyran mythology is the Lyran-Draconian War, described as a series of [devastating conflicts](https://ascensionglossary.com/index.php/Lyran_Wars) between the peaceful Lyran humanoids and the aggressive Alpha Draconian reptilian race. According to these narratives, the Draconians invaded the Lyran Empire in a catastrophic assault, destroying three of 14 planets in the Lyran system. The war culminated in a pyrrhic victory and [uneasy truce](https://newdisclosureencyclopedia.fandom.com/wiki/Lyran-Draconian_War), with the founding of the Lyran Council and the Galactic Federation — an interstellar governing body that allegedly still mediates between humanoid and reptilian factions. They are said to be independent, adventurous, natural leaders with a deep love of freedom and a feline quality to their energy — connecting back to the claim that some original Lyrans were feline-humanoid hybrids. These concepts derive entirely from channeled communications and New Age spiritual teachings, with no scientific documentation or astronomical evidence supporting the existence of civilizations in the Lyra constellation.

## 11. Blue Avians

The Blue Avians are a claimed extraterrestrial species whose entire public narrative originates from a single source: Corey Goode, a Texas-based individual who emerged in the UFO community around 2014-2015 claiming to be a whistleblower from a covert "Secret Space Program" (SSP). Goode alleges he was recruited at age 6 as an "intuitive empath" into a program called "[20 and Back](https://www.equip.org/articles/corey-goode-time-traveling-secret-space-program-whistleblower/)," in which participants serve 20 years in secret space operations before being age-regressed and returned to the point of departure with their memories wiped. Within this framework, Goode claims he encountered the Blue Avians — described as humanlike beings approximately 8 feet tall with [blue and purple feathers](https://ascensionworks.tv/blueavians/), bird-like features, and indigo-blue skin — as part of an alliance called the Sphere Being Alliance, which allegedly placed enormous, cloaked spheres throughout the solar system. His primary Blue Avian contacts are identified as Ra-Tear-Eir and Ra-Rain-Eir, and their core message emphasizes self-forgiveness, karmic release, raising collective consciousness, and [service to others](https://spherebeingalliance.com/faqs) — themes closely mirroring the philosophical content of *The Ra Material* (commonly known as *The Law of One*), a series of channeled sessions conducted between 1981 and 1984 by physicist Don Elkins, Carla Rueckert, and Jim McCarty with an entity identifying itself as "Ra."

The explicit connection between the Blue Avians and the Law of One is central to Goode's narrative. He claims the Blue Avians asked him to read the Law of One material, and when the SSP Alliance directly asked them ["Are you the Ra from the Law of One series?"](https://ascensionworks.tv/blueavians/) their answer was "I am Ra" — the same phrase used by the entity in the original 1981 channeling sessions. In late 2016, Goode reported that Ra-Teir-Eir's group consciousness, which they described as sixth-density, was [the same source](https://spherebeingalliance.com/blog/corey-goode-intel-update-part-1-aug-2016.html) as the Ra Contact. The Blue Avians' message warns that they had tried to deliver this information three previous times in human history but it was distorted, and they specifically warned that the information was not to become a cult or religious movement. Goode's claims gained massive visibility through the Gaia TV series *Cosmic Disclosure*, co-hosted with author David Wilcock. However, the entire Blue Avian narrative collapsed under legal and evidentiary scrutiny: Goode sued Gaia in 2020, and Gaia countersued, accusing him of [defamation and sabotage](https://www.thedailybeast.com/ufo-community-explodes-in-lawsuit-drama-accusations-of-luciferianism/). Most devastatingly, during sworn deposition in the Goode v. Gaia case, [Goode admitted under oath](https://ohnopodcast.com/investigations/2024/4/15/ross-and-carrie-hear-out-corey-goode-devastating-deposition-edition) that he never went to space, never participated in a "20 and Back" program, and was not communicating with aliens — characterizing his claims as creative intellectual property. He also filed [trademarks](https://dukebrickhouse.substack.com/p/corey-goode-thinks-he-owns-the-secret) on phrases like "Secret Space Program," "20 & Back," and "Blue Avians" — an incongruous action if these phenomena actually existed independently of his narrative.

## 12. Anshar

The Anshar are a claimed inner-Earth civilization introduced entirely within Corey Goode's Secret Space Program narrative, and they represent one of the most elaborate elements of his cosmology — which, as noted in the Blue Avians section above, Goode admitted under oath was creative intellectual property rather than factual testimony. Within the narrative as originally presented, the Anshar are described as a [fourth-density civilization](https://exopolitics.org/inner-earth-beings-take-first-step-to-openly-reveal-themselves-to-humanity/) that has existed for approximately 18 million years in vast cities within the Earth's interior. They trace their lineage back to Earth itself rather than to another star system, and Goode describes them as appearing human — tall, slender beings with elongated skulls and pale, luminous skin. His primary Anshar contact is an entity named [Ka Aree](https://spherebeingalliance.com/blog/transcript-ets-and-inner-earth-beings.html), described as an Anshar Priestess who serves as his chief liaison with the civilization. Goode claims to have been physically transported to Anshar cities in the Earth's interior, describing domed caverns illuminated by a kind of ambient light, with crystalline structures, advanced technology, and a society organized around spiritual practice and temporal management.

The most distinctive claim about the Anshar is that they are [humanity's distant future descendants](https://ufos-disclosure.blogspot.com/2019/04/preparing-for-disclosure-and-solar.html), who traveled backward in time to ensure their own timeline's survival — making them simultaneously ancient inner-Earth dwellers and future humans. This temporal paradox is central to the Anshar narrative: they allegedly exist in the past to protect a future that depends on specific events in the present unfolding correctly. Goode reported that the Anshar had maintained a strict policy of separation from surface humanity for millennia, but around 2016, their leaders decided to change this policy and begin [preparations to openly reveal themselves](https://michaelsalla.com/2016/04/27/inner-earth-beings-take-first-step-to-openly-reveal-themselves-to-humanity/) to the surface population, with Anshar city inhabitants reportedly feeling excitement over this shift. Ka Aree allegedly communicated to Goode about Earth's impending transition into [fourth-density consciousness](https://www.ashtarcommandcrew.net/profiles/blogs/accelerating-ascension-transitioning-into-4th-density), a process connected to solar energetic events and the broader ascension timeline. The Anshar narrative builds on a long tradition of hollow earth or inner earth mythology — from Edmund Halley's 17th-century hollow earth hypothesis through Admiral Byrd's alleged diary entries to the Agartha legends of Theosophical tradition. However, modern seismology has comprehensively mapped Earth's interior through seismic wave analysis, demonstrating a solid iron-nickel inner core, liquid outer core, mantle, and crust — a structure fundamentally incompatible with vast hollow interior spaces housing civilizations. Combined with Goode's legal admissions that his claims constitute creative IP, the Anshar remain a fictional construct within a larger narrative framework.

## 13. Ashtar Command

The Ashtar Command represents one of the oldest continuously active claimed extraterrestrial contact narratives, originating on July 18, 1952, when American UFO contactee [George Van Tassel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ashtar_Sheran) — an aeronautics engineer who had previously worked for Howard Hughes, Douglas Aircraft, and Lockheed — reported receiving a telepathic transmission from an entity identifying itself as "Ashtar, Commandant of Station Schare." Van Tassel, who lived beneath a massive freestanding boulder called Giant Rock in the Mojave Desert near Landers, California, claimed the contact was not a traditional face-to-face encounter but rather [telepathic reception of transmissions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ashtar_Command) sent through advanced extraterrestrial communications devices. The initial Ashtar messages contained apocalyptic warnings focused on the hydrogen bomb, claiming that the Ashtar Galactic Command had entered the solar system specifically to warn humanity against nuclear weapons testing, which they said threatened to destabilize not just Earth but the fabric of space itself. Van Tassel organized the [Giant Rock Spacecraft Convention](https://www.encyclopedia.com/science/encyclopedias-almanacs-transcripts-and-maps/van-tassel-george-w-1910-1978), beginning in the spring of 1953 and continuing annually for over 24 years, which became the largest UFO contactee gathering in the world — attracting up to [11,000 attendees in 1959](https://wrldrels.org/2016/12/05/ashtar-command/). At these conventions, Van Tassel and other contactees held weekly channeling sessions where audience members could ask questions and receive "channeled responses" from supposed extraterrestrials, with most of the early Ashtar messages being first presented to the public at these events.

The Ashtar movement evolved significantly after Van Tassel, particularly through the work of Thelma B. Turrell, known publicly as [Tuella](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ashtar_(channeling)), who emerged in the 1980s as Ashtar's primary spokesperson. Tuella founded Guardian Action International and Guardian Action Publications, producing books including *Ashtar: Revealing the Secret Identity of the Forces of Light*, which repositioned the Ashtar narrative from physical UFO contact toward a more [theosophical and spiritualized framework](https://www.encyclopedia.com/science/encyclopedias-almanacs-transcripts-and-maps/ashtar). This shift was partly driven by the repeated failure of specific prophecies: early Ashtar messages had predicted imminent spacecraft landings, civilizations flourishing on other planets in our solar system, and physical rescue operations for faithful humans — none of which materialized. After these [prophetic failures](https://wrldrels.org/2016/12/05/ashtar-command/), the messaging evolved to emphasize spiritual liberation, dimensional ascension, and the religious teachings of Ashtar rather than physical events. Robert Short (also known as Bill Rose), editor of the 1950s UFO magazine *Interplanetary News Digest*, also began his own organization called "Ashtar Command." By the mid-1950s, the concept of Ashtar and a galactic law enforcement agency preparing an imminent rescue of humanity had become well-established across the contactee subculture. Academic researcher Andreas Grunschloss has described the modern Ashtar Command as a [worldwide network of loosely organized groups](https://wrldrels.org/2016/12/05/ashtar-command/), comparing much of the Ashtar channeling to cargo cults due to the blending of spiritual ascension promises with expectations of alien technologies and ecologically harmless energies. The Ashtar Command community remains active today through websites, online channeling groups, and social media, making it one of the longest-running UFO religious movements in history — spanning over seven decades from Van Tassel's first claimed transmission.

## 14. Tall Greys (Eban) / Project Serpo

The Tall Greys, sometimes referred to as the Eban (or Eben), represent the alleged leadership caste of the Grey alien hierarchy, and their most detailed portrayal comes through the highly contested [Project Serpo](https://www.gaia.com/article/project-serpo-zeta-reticuli-exchange-program) narrative. Project Serpo is an alleged top-secret exchange program in which twelve American military personnel — ten men and two women — were supposedly selected in the early 1960s and sent aboard an Eben spacecraft in 1965 to the planet Serpo, orbiting a binary star system in Zeta Reticuli, approximately 39 light-years from Earth, where they reportedly lived for over a decade. The claims surfaced online in the early 2000s through a series of [anonymous emails](https://www.serpo.org/project-serpo-a-secret-exchange-between-aliens-and-humans/) sent to UFO researchers, allegedly from a retired senior U.S. government official using the pseudonym "Anonymous." According to these "Serpo Files," the exchange program was initiated following the recovery of a surviving Eben from a UFO crash (often linked to the 1947 Roswell or 1949 Kingman incidents), and the surviving alien facilitated communication between its civilization and the U.S. government, ultimately leading to the agreement.

The Ebens are described as small humanoid beings standing approximately four feet tall, with [large craniums, grayish-brown skin](https://alessandragemmo.com/the-eben-species-alien-diplomats-or-cold-observers/), and deep-set almond-shaped black eyes — aligning closely with standard Grey descriptions but with some distinguishing features. Their society on Serpo is depicted as [strictly organized yet non-hierarchical](https://alessandragemmo.com/the-eben-species-alien-diplomats-or-cold-observers/), with no concept of money, trade, or crime. Violence was reportedly almost unheard of in their civilization, and they had leadership and an army but were never observed using weapons. Their language was based on tonal and symbolic patterns unlike any on Earth, making communication extremely difficult for the human exchange team. Within the broader Grey hierarchy as described in conspiracy literature, the Tall Greys or Eban leaders are positioned above the smaller worker Greys — where the small Greys function as biological drones or subordinates while the Tall Greys direct operations, negotiate with governments, and make strategic decisions. The Serpo Files present the Ebens not as malevolent abductors but as [coldly analytical, highly structured](https://alessandragemmo.com/the-eben-species-alien-diplomats-or-cold-observers/) beings — alien diplomats or cold observers rather than outright aggressors. However, the entire Project Serpo narrative rests on anonymous, unverifiable claims with [no physical evidence](https://www.gaia.com/article/project-serpo-zeta-reticuli-exchange-program) supporting the project's existence, no confirmed identities of the alleged twelve exchange participants, and significant internal contradictions that have led many even within the UFO community to suspect the entire narrative is either disinformation or deliberate fiction.

## 15. Feline Beings / Lion People

Feline beings, variously known as Lion People, Cat People, or by the name Urmah, represent one of the more distinctive claimed extraterrestrial types in New Age cosmology — a race of large, bipedal, feline-humanoid beings whose mythology is deeply intertwined with both the Lyran origin story and ancient Egyptian religion. According to channeled accounts, the [Feline race originates from the Lyra constellation](https://mystfiles.com/the-feline-extraterrestrials-galactic-warriors-of-lyra-and-sirius/), specifically from a planet called Avyon in the Vega star system, making them contemporaries and kin of the humanoid Lyrans. They are described as large beings standing 3 to 4 meters tall (roughly 10 to 13 feet), with fully developed feline features including [fur ranging from golden-brown to pure white](https://www.astrallara.com/blog-1-1/the-urmah-ancient-lyran-feline-beings), leonine or tigerlike faces, manes, and retractable claws. In the galactic narrative, the feline beings are characterized as formidable warriors who serve as one of the principal militia forces in the alleged galactic hierarchy, yet are simultaneously described as deeply spiritual and pacific in their current era — having evolved beyond their earlier warlike tendencies. Within the Swaruu of Taygeta channeling framework (a series of alleged communications from [Pleiadian beings](https://swaruu.org/transcripts/extraterrestrial-races-urmahs-feline-race-swaruu-extraterrestrial-pleiadian-communication) that has developed a large online following), the Urmah are described as maintaining a close friendship with the Taygetans, reportedly keeping domestic cats aboard their ships as companions — offered as an explanation for why cats hold such a prominent place in human affection and mythology.

The strongest cultural anchor for feline alien theories lies in ancient Egyptian religion, where cat and lion worship was extraordinarily prominent. The goddess [Sekhmet](https://cryptidz.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_Aliens), depicted as a woman with a lioness's head, was one of the most powerful deities in the Egyptian pantheon — her name means "The Powerful One," and she embodied both destructive warfare and protective healing. Alternative theorists and ancient astronaut proponents argue that Sekhmet was not merely a mythological figure but a [representation of actual feline extraterrestrial visitors](https://otherworldlyoracle.com/lyran-starseeds-markings-origins-gods/) worshipped as deities by the Egyptians. Similarly, the goddess Bastet — originally depicted as a fierce lioness and later as a domestic cat — is cited as embodying [Lyran feline energy](https://www.sacredlotus.eu/blogs/news/lyran-starseeds-the-feline-lineage-ancient-wisdom-from-the-stars), representing protection, sensuality, music, and divine feminine power. The lion-headed god Mahes, the Great Sphinx of Giza (which some alternative theorists argue originally had a fully leonine head before being recarved), and the ubiquity of cat mummification in ancient Egypt are all marshaled as evidence that the Egyptians were [memorializing contact with feline extraterrestrials](https://cryptidz.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_Aliens). Mainstream Egyptologists, of course, explain feline worship through the observable behavior of cats and lions in the Egyptian environment — cats controlled vermin that threatened grain stores, while lions were apex predators that inspired awe — without requiring extraterrestrial intervention. The feline being concept exists primarily within New Age and metaphysical communities, with no archaeological or scientific evidence supporting the existence of bipedal feline extraterrestrials. Nevertheless, the Lyran feline narrative has proven particularly enduring and emotionally resonant, perhaps because it offers a cosmic explanation for humanity's unusually deep and cross-cultural bond with cats.
