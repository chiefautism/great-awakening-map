## 1. Inner Earth Civilizations -- Agartha and the Hollow Earth

### Historical origins

The idea of a hollow Earth has roots stretching back centuries. In 1818, American Army officer **John Cleves Symmes Jr.** publicly proposed that the Earth consisted of a hollow shell roughly 1,300 km thick, with openings approximately 2,300 km across at both poles and four concentric inner shells. The notion was tentatively challenged as early as 1740 by Pierre Bouguer, then definitively refuted by Charles Hutton's Schiehallion experiment around 1774, yet it persisted in popular imagination well into the 19th century.

### Agartha

**Agartha** (also spelled Agharta, Agharti) is a legendary kingdom said to exist on the inner surface of the Earth. The term was introduced by French colonial official **Louis Jacolliot** in his 1873 book *Les fils de Dieu*, where he claimed access to 15,000-year-old Indian manuscripts describing an ancient city called "Asgartha." The concept was later developed by **Alexandre Saint-Yves d'Alveydre** in *Mission de l'Inde* (1886), who described it as a hidden utopia ruled by a "King of the World" (Brahâtma).

In the 20th century, writer **Walter Siegmeister** (pen name Raymond Bernard) cemented the association between Agartha and hollow Earth theory. Many Theosophical offshoots adopted the idea of a "Grand Lodge of Agartha" inhabited by ascended masters possessing telepathic abilities and extraordinary longevity.

### Scientific status

Modern geology has conclusively ruled out the hollow Earth hypothesis. Seismological data, gravitational measurements, and the known density of the Earth confirm a solid mantle and core structure.

## 3. Antarctica Conspiracy Theories

### The pyramid theory

When satellite images of pyramidal peaks in the **Ellsworth Mountains** circulated on social media beginning in 2016, claims spread that these were artificial pyramids built by an ancient civilization, aliens, or Nazi remnants. Geologists explain these as **glacial horns** (also called nunataks) -- a well-understood landform created when multiple glaciers erode a mountain from different sides, producing a pyramidal shape.

### Ancient civilization claims

Modern ice-core data confirms that Antarctica has not been free of ice for well over **10 million years**, long before anatomically modern humans existed (~300,000 years ago). This makes theories of ancient human civilizations on Antarctica scientifically untenable.

### Nazi base theory

The Nazi base theory has a slender factual kernel: Germany did send an expedition in 1938-39 (see Neuschwabenland section below). However, **no permanent base was ever established**, and Germany abandoned its Antarctic territorial claim upon its defeat in 1945.

### The Corey Goode narrative

Corey Goode claims that a "Pre-Adamite" civilization with 10-12 foot tall elongated-skull beings was flash-frozen in Antarctica approximately 12,800 years ago during a sudden pole shift, and that three oval-shaped motherships (each ~30 miles in diameter) lie buried beneath the ice. According to exopolitics promoter **Michael Salla**, multiple insiders have visited ruins being excavated under the ice. None of these claims have any independent verification.

## 4. Operation Highjump (1946-1947)

### Verified history

**Operation HIGHJUMP** (officially: United States Navy Antarctic Developments Program, 1946-1947) was a major U.S. Navy expedition organized by **Rear Admiral Richard E. Byrd Jr.** and commanded by **Rear Admiral Richard H. Cruzen**. It launched on 26 August 1946 and concluded in late February 1947.

**Scale:** 4,700 men, 13 ships (including the aircraft carrier USS *Philippine Sea*), and 33 aircraft.

**Stated objectives:** Train naval personnel under frigid conditions; test ships, planes, and helicopters in Antarctic environments; develop techniques for establishing air bases; conduct aerial photography and geographic surveys. The expedition completed 28 photographic flights, capturing over 70,000 photos covering 1.5 million square miles. A classified secondary objective was to bolster U.S. territorial claims in Antarctica amid emerging Cold War competition with the Soviet Union.

### Conspiracy theories

**The Nazi battle narrative:** Conspiracy theorists claim Byrd's fleet was actually an invasion force sent to destroy a secret Nazi Antarctic stronghold, and that the expedition was cut short after being attacked by advanced Nazi flying saucers emerging from beneath the ice. There is no military record or credible testimony supporting a battle.

**The misquoted interview:** The conspiracy version traces to a 1947 interview Byrd gave to the Chilean newspaper *El Mercurio*, in which he warned that the U.S. should prepare for attacks by aircraft capable of flying pole-to-pole at high speed. This was a Cold War warning about **Soviet bombers** using polar routes -- not a reference to Nazi UFOs. The statement was mistranslated and taken out of context by later conspiracy authors.

**The "missing diary":** A book titled *The Missing Diary of Admiral Richard E. Byrd* describes Byrd flying into the hollow Earth and discovering a crystal city inhabited by beings called the "Arianni." This was not written by Byrd. It was authored by **Walter Siegmeister** (pen name Raymond Bernard), a hollow Earth enthusiast. The **Byrd Polar and Climate Research Center** at Ohio State University has stated that no records in their archive support these claims.

### Why the expedition ended early

The expedition was cut short due to the onset of Antarctic winter (pack ice) and the loss of several aircraft, well-documented operational hazards -- not an alien or Nazi battle.

## 5. Neuschwabenland (New Swabia)

### Verified history

**New Swabia** (German: *Neuschwabenland*) refers to the area of Antarctica explored by the **Third German Antarctic Expedition (1938-1939)**, led by Captain **Alfred Ritscher** aboard the MS *Schwabenland*. The ship departed Hamburg on 17 December 1938 with 82 crew members.

**Primary objective:** Scout a location for a German whaling station to reduce dependence on Norwegian whale oil imports. A secondary, secret military mission was to assess the islands of Trindade and Martim Vaz as potential naval bases.

**Activities:** Seven photographic survey flights were conducted using two Dornier Wal seaplanes (*Passat* and *Boreas*). About a dozen 1.2-meter aluminum arrows tipped with steel cones and embossed with swastikas were airdropped onto the ice to symbolically mark territory.

**Outcome:** Germany issued a decree about establishing an Antarctic sector called "New Swabia" in August 1939 but **never filed an official territorial claim under international law**. No permanent settlement, whaling station, or military base was ever constructed. The claim was abandoned entirely in 1945.

### Conspiracy theory

Conspiracy narratives assert that a massive hidden military base was built in Neuschwabenland during or after the war, to which high-ranking Nazis, scientists, and military units fled with advanced technology (flying saucers, Die Glocke). The narrative claims the base survived the war and continues to operate. There is no evidence for any of this; logistically, Nazi Germany lacked the resources and supply chains to maintain a secret Antarctic fortress while simultaneously losing the war in Europe.

## 6. The Nazi Bell (Die Glocke)

### Origin of the story

**Die Glocke** ("The Bell") is a purported top-secret Nazi device first described by Polish journalist **Igor Witkowski** in his 2000 book *Prawda o Wunderwaffe* ("The Truth About the Wonder Weapon"). According to Witkowski, in August 1997 an unnamed Polish intelligence contact showed him classified transcripts from Nazi-era documents. Witkowski says he was allowed to transcribe but not copy the documents -- and no independent researcher has ever seen the originals.

The story was popularized internationally by British aerospace journalist **Nick Cook** in *The Hunt for Zero Point* (2001), and further expanded by **Joseph P. Farrell** and others who connected it to Nazi occultism and antigravity research.

### Alleged description

Die Glocke is described as a bell-shaped device made of hard, heavy metal, approximately 9 feet wide and 12-15 feet tall. It supposedly contained two counter-rotating cylinders filled with a violet-hued metallic liquid code-named "Xerum 525" (sometimes speculated to be mercury isotopes). When activated, it allegedly produced effects including antigravity, radiation sickness in nearby personnel (several scientists reportedly died), and possibly distortions in the flow of time.

The experiments were said to have been conducted at a facility called **Der Riese** ("The Giant") near the Wenceslaus mine, close to the Czech border, under the direction of SS General **Hans Kammler**.

### Skeptical assessment

Former aerospace scientist **David Myhra** and skeptic **Jason Colavito** have expressed doubt that the device ever existed. Colavito calls it "recycled reflection" of 1960s rumors about Nazi occult science and "a device few outside of fringe culture think actually existed. In short, it looks to be a hoax, or at least a wild exaggeration." The sole chain of evidence traces back to Witkowski's unverifiable claim about documents shown to him by an anonymous source.

## 7. Haunebu -- Alleged Nazi Flying Saucers

### The claims

"Haunebu" is one of several alleged designations for Nazi flying disc aircraft, alongside names like Vril, Andromeda-Gerat, and Flugscheibe. The narrative claims that German engineers, sometimes in partnership with the **Vril Society** and the **Thule Society**, developed antigravity flying saucer prototypes during World War II. According to the more elaborate versions, after Germany's defeat, the technology and its creators retreated to a base in Antarctica.

### Origins

The earliest non-fiction assertion of Nazi flying saucers appears to have been an article in the Italian newspaper **Il Giornale d'Italia** in early 1950, which claimed that "types of flying discs were designed and studied in Germany and Italy as early as 1942." By the 1960s, fringe authors had woven these claims into narratives involving the occult, alien contact, and hollow Earth sanctuaries.

### Evidence

When Allied forces occupied Germany at the end of WWII, **no physical evidence of flying disc aircraft was found** -- no prototypes, no engines, no hangars. The only "evidence" consists of alleged design documents and testimonies that emerged decades later and cannot be authenticated. The Haunebu exists primarily in model kits, video games, and conspiracy literature.

Germany did produce genuinely innovative aircraft designs (Me 262 jet fighter, Ho 229 flying wing, various rocket programs), but none of these were disc-shaped antigravity craft.

## 8. Atlantis, Lemuria, and Mu

### Atlantis

**Atlantis** originates entirely from two dialogues by the Greek philosopher **Plato** -- *Timaeus* and *Critias* (c. 360 BC). In Plato's account, Atlantis was a powerful naval empire located "beyond the Pillars of Hercules" (Strait of Gibraltar) that conquered much of Europe and Libya before launching an ill-fated campaign against Athens. After losing divine favor, the island sank into the Atlantic "in a single day and night of misfortune."

Plato attributed the story to the Athenian statesman **Solon**, who supposedly heard it from Egyptian priests. Modern philologists and classicists broadly agree the account is **fictional** -- a literary device Plato used to explore themes of hubris, ideal governance, and divine punishment. Possible real-world inspirations include the eruption of Thera (Santorini) around 1600 BC, the Sea Peoples invasions, the failed Athenian invasion of Sicily (415-413 BC), and the destruction of the city of Helike by earthquake and tsunami in 373 BC.

Despite scholarly consensus, Atlantis has become a cultural touchstone. Thousands of books have proposed locations from the Mediterranean to the Caribbean to Antarctica.

### Lemuria

**Lemuria** was proposed in 1864 by zoologist **Philip Sclater** as a hypothetical land bridge in the Indian Ocean to explain why lemur fossils appeared in Madagascar and India but not in Africa or the Middle East. In 1870, biologist **Ernst Haeckel** suggested Lemuria might be the ancestral homeland of humanity. The hypothesis was scientifically abandoned after the acceptance of **plate tectonics and continental drift** in the 20th century, which explained the fossil distribution without requiring a sunken continent.

However, **Helena Blavatsky** and the Theosophical movement adopted Lemuria as a real lost continent in *The Secret Doctrine* (1888), claiming it was inhabited by a "Third Root Race" of ape-like giants. This esoteric version lives on in New Age circles.

### Mu

**Mu** was proposed by 19th-century French archaeologist **Charles-Etienne Brasseur de Bourbourg** based on his (mis)interpretation of Mayan hieroglyphics, and was later elaborated by British-American writer **James Churchward** in a series of books beginning with *The Lost Continent of Mu* (1926). Churchward placed Mu in the Pacific Ocean and claimed it was the cradle of human civilization, destroyed about 12,000 years ago.

Like Lemuria, the existence of Mu is incompatible with plate tectonics. There is no geological evidence for a sunken Pacific continent.

## 9. The Vril Society

### The fiction behind the name

"Vril" originates from the 1871 science fiction novel *The Coming Race* by **Edward Bulwer-Lytton**, which describes a subterranean master race (the Vril-ya) that wields a powerful energy force called "vril." The novel was intended as satire, but some occultists took it literally.

### The alleged society

The existence of a "Vril Society" was first claimed by French authors **Louis Pauwels** and **Jacques Bergier** in their 1960 book *Le Matin des Magiciens* (*The Morning of the Magicians*). They alleged that the Nazi leadership attempted to forge alliances with supernatural powers through this secret society.

Historian **Nicholas Goodrick-Clarke** acknowledged the existence of a small group in pre-war Germany that genuinely believed in vril energy, but he **denied it played any significant political role**. Extensive historical research has found no evidence that the Vril Society (if it existed at all) exerted meaningful influence on Hitler or the NSDAP.

### The Thule Society (actual historical organization)

Unlike the Vril Society, the **Thule Society** (Thule-Gesellschaft) was a documented organization, founded in Munich in 1918 by **Rudolf von Sebottendorf**. It combined Germanic nationalism, antisemitism, and occult interests. Several early Nazi Party members had connections to the Thule Society, but mainstream historians regard its influence on Nazism as limited and indirect.

In conspiracy narratives, the Vril Society is often depicted as an inner circle or mystical offshoot of the Thule Society -- the Thule Society handled politics while the Vril Society pursued the "deepest occult secrets" and advanced technology, including flying saucers and contact with extraterrestrials from the Aldebaran star system.

### Scholarly consensus

Multiple scholars attribute the modern "Nazi Mysteries" genre to the 1960 Pauwels/Bergier book. Historical research has concluded that occult groups did not exert significant influence on Nazi policy or technology development.

## 10. Pre-Adamites -- Elongated Skull Giants and Cabal Origin Theory

### Historical theological concept

**Pre-Adamism** is the theological idea that humans (or intelligent beings) existed before the biblical Adam. The concept was formally articulated by French millenarian **Isaac La Peyrere** in his 1655 work *Prae-Adamitae*. During the Enlightenment, pre-Adamism was used to challenge biblical chronology; in the 19th century it was co-opted by racial theorists to argue for polygenism (multiple origins of human races) and white supremacy.

### Elongated skulls

The famous elongated skulls most often cited by conspiracy theorists come from the **Paracas culture** of Peru (c. 750 BC -- AD 100). Like many Andean cultures, the Paracas practiced **artificial cranial modification** -- the intentional reshaping of an infant's skull through binding. This is a well-documented anthropological practice found across many cultures worldwide (Maya, Chinook, Alans, Huns, and others).

Conspiracy theorists, notably **Brien Foerster**, claim that some Paracas skulls show cranial volume too large to be explained by binding alone and must therefore represent a separate (possibly alien) species. Mainstream physical anthropologists have rejected these claims, noting that cranial deformation can indeed increase apparent vault volume.

### The conspiracy narrative

In the conspiracy version promoted through Corey Goode, David Wilcock, and exopolitics circles, Pre-Adamites are presented as the original ruling elite -- 10-12 foot tall beings with elongated skulls who arrived on Earth approximately 55,000 years ago from another planet. After a cataclysm flash-froze Antarctica roughly 12,800 years ago, their surviving hybrid bloodlines allegedly became the ruling "cabal" families that control the world today. The elongated skulls found in Peru, Egypt, and elsewhere are cited as evidence of this bloodline.

### Giant skeleton claims

Claims of "giant skeletons" being found across North America were debunked as early as 1934 by **Ales Hrdlicka**, curator of anthropology at the Smithsonian Institution. The debunking itself spawned a secondary conspiracy theory -- that the Smithsonian has been actively destroying evidence of giants.

## 11. Stasis Giants -- Red-Haired Giants in Stasis Chambers

### The claims

The "stasis giants" narrative asserts that ancient giants -- typically described as 12+ feet tall with red hair, red beards, and pale skin -- preserved themselves in technologically advanced stasis chambers thousands of years ago and are now being discovered (and sometimes awakening) around the world.

The primary source is again **Corey Goode**, who discussed the concept on *Cosmic Disclosure* (August 4, 2015). Goode claims he accessed information about sleeping giants on "smart-glass pads" during his alleged covert service. According to the narrative, these giants belonged to highly evolved ancient civilizations that used **crystal-based technology** to slow time within the chambers, effectively suspending biological processes.

### Alleged locations

Proponents claim stasis chambers have been found in locations including:
- Utah (United States)
- Iran
- Various sites in the Middle East
- Under the Egyptian pyramids

### The coverup narrative

**Michael Salla** (Exopolitics.org) has written that "awakened stasis giants" have been secretly located and imprisoned by a "global elite" that does not want humanity to learn the truth. These giants are sometimes identified with the biblical **Nephilim** or with the Sumerian **Anunnaki**, and occasionally linked to the figure of **Gilgamesh**.

### Red-haired giant folklore

The notion of red-haired giants has separate roots in actual Native American folklore. The **Paiute** people have an oral tradition about the **Si-Te-Cah**, a race of red-haired cannibals who were driven into **Lovelock Cave** in Nevada and exterminated. In 1911, guano miners did find human remains and artifacts in Lovelock Cave, though archaeologists identified these as belonging to normal-sized humans of the local population.

### Assessment

No stasis chamber technology has ever been demonstrated or recovered. No credible physical evidence of 12-foot-tall humanoids has been produced. The narrative relies entirely on anonymous insider testimony and unverifiable claims.

## 12. Area 51

### Verified history

**Area 51** is an active U.S. Air Force military installation located at **Groom Lake** in the Nevada Test and Training Range, approximately 83 miles northwest of Las Vegas. The U.S. government did not formally acknowledge its existence until **2013**, when declassified CIA documents confirmed the site.

**Origin:** In 1955, the CIA selected the remote Groom Lake site to test the **U-2 spy plane** prototype. The location was chosen for its remoteness, clear weather, existing (though basic) runway from nuclear testing days, and proximity to the Nevada Test Site.

**Known aircraft tested at Area 51:**
- **U-2** reconnaissance aircraft (1955+)
- **A-12 OXCART** (Mach 3+ reconnaissance plane, predecessor to the SR-71 Blackbird)
- **F-117 Nighthawk** stealth fighter
- **Have Blue** (stealth technology demonstrator)
- Various captured Soviet aircraft for evaluation (under programs like HAVE DOUGHNUT)

### Why UFO sightings proliferated

Test flights of the U-2 and subsequent aircraft accounted for many of the UFO sightings in the area. The U-2 could reach altitudes above 60,000 feet -- far higher than any other aircraft at the time -- and its silver wings reflected sunlight in ways that looked otherworldly to observers below.

### The Bob Lazar claims (1989)

The alien conspiracy theory surrounding Area 51 exploded in **1989** when **Bob Lazar** gave an interview with Las Vegas TV station KLAS, claiming he had worked at a facility called "S-4" near Papoose Lake (south of Groom Lake). Lazar said his job was to reverse-engineer alien propulsion systems from recovered extraterrestrial spacecraft. He described the craft as using "Element 115" (later synthesized as moscovium, which does not behave as Lazar described).

Lazar's credentials were quickly challenged: MIT and Caltech said they had no record of him, and Los Alamos National Laboratory denied he had worked there (though a phone directory listing his name was later found, suggesting at minimum some lower-level contractor role).

### Expanded conspiracy claims

Beyond Lazar, Area 51 conspiracy theories include:
- Storage and study of crashed alien craft (especially Roswell debris)
- Meetings with extraterrestrial beings
- Development of weather control, time travel, and teleportation technology
- Exotic energy weapons

None of these claims have been substantiated. The 2013 declassification confirmed the base's role in flight testing, not extraterrestrial research.

## 13. D.U.M.B.S. -- Deep Underground Military Bases

### Real underground facilities

Underground military installations genuinely exist and some are publicly acknowledged:

- **Cheyenne Mountain Complex** (Colorado) -- NORAD command center, built inside a granite mountain
- **Raven Rock Mountain Complex** (Pennsylvania) -- alternate Pentagon command facility
- **Mount Weather Emergency Operations Center** (Virginia) -- FEMA continuity-of-government facility
- **Strategic missile silos** across the western United States

The U.S. government has openly discussed underground continuity-of-government bunkers since the Cold War.

### The conspiracy version

The conspiracy theory, using the acronym **D.U.M.B.S.**, dramatically expands the concept, claiming a vast **network of 100+ secret underground cities** connected by high-speed maglev trains, funded by "black budgets," and serving as hubs for:
- Extraterrestrial collaboration and alien technology reverse-engineering
- Genetic experimentation and human-alien hybridization
- Secret prisons and trafficking operations
- New World Order command infrastructure

### Phil Schneider

The D.U.M.B.S. legend gained traction in the 1990s through **Phil Schneider** (1947-1996), who claimed to be a geologist/structural engineer who worked on constructing underground bases. His central claim was that while drilling at **Dulce, New Mexico** in 1979, his team broke into a cavern occupied by tall grey aliens, resulting in a firefight that killed 60 humans and several aliens. Schneider said he was wounded in the encounter, showing scars and missing fingers as evidence.

Schneider was found dead on January 17, 1996, in his Wilsonville, Oregon apartment. The official cause was suicide by asphyxiation. Supporters allege he was murdered for his disclosures. During his life and after his death, **no evidence has been presented confirming he ever worked for any government organization** in the capacity he described.

### Mainstream assessment

While real underground military facilities exist, no credible evidence supports the existence of a conspiracy-level secret network of underground cities with alien labs and maglev trains. The logistical requirements for such infrastructure (ventilation, power, construction, waste removal, staffing) would be extraordinarily difficult to conceal.

## 14. Dulce Base

### The claims

**Dulce Base** is an alleged jointly-operated human-alien underground facility said to exist beneath **Archuleta Mesa** on the Jicarilla Apache Indian Reservation near the town of Dulce, New Mexico (population ~2,600).

The base is described as extending **two miles underground** with **seven levels**, each devoted to increasingly disturbing activities:
- Upper levels: Security, communications, human staff housing
- Middle levels: Human-alien collaborative labs
- Lower levels (especially "Nightmare Hall," Level 6): Genetic experiments, human-alien hybridization, holding tanks for experimental subjects

### Key figures

**Paul Bennewitz** (1927-2003): An Albuquerque electronics businessman who, starting in 1979, became convinced he was intercepting electronic communications from alien spacecraft near Kirtland Air Force Base. By the early 1980s, he had developed a detailed theory about an underground alien base at Dulce. It was later revealed that **Air Force Office of Special Investigations (AFOSI) agent Richard Doty** had actively fed Bennewitz disinformation as part of a counterintelligence operation to discredit him and distract from classified programs at Kirtland. Bennewitz eventually suffered a mental breakdown.

**John Lear**: UFOlogist and pilot who claimed in 1987 to have independent confirmations of the base's existence.

**Thomas Allen LeVesque** ("Jason Bishop III"): Influenced by Lear's claims, LeVesque produced detailed accounts of the Dulce Base -- and **later admitted to fabricating stories** about it.

**Phil Schneider**: Claimed to have helped construct the base in 1979 and to have survived the firefight with aliens described in the D.U.M.B.S. section above.

### Assessment

Political scientist **Michael Barkun** has noted that Cold War underground missile installations in the area gave "superficial plausibility" to the rumor, making Dulce Base an "attractive legend" within UFOlogy. The existence of the base remains **entirely unproven**. The primary originator (Bennewitz) was a confirmed target of military disinformation, and one of the key elaborators (LeVesque) admitted to fabrication.

## 15. Sasquatch / Bigfoot as Inner Earth Beings

### Mainstream cryptozoology view

Bigfoot or Sasquatch is a cryptid -- an animal whose existence is claimed but not proven by mainstream science. The conventional cryptozoological hypothesis holds that if Sasquatch exists, it is a **relict hominid** -- perhaps a surviving population of *Gigantopithecus blacki* (an extinct great ape) or an unknown primate species.

### The interdimensional / inner Earth theory

A subset of Bigfoot researchers have proposed more exotic explanations:

**Interdimensional hypothesis:** Paranormal investigator **Jon-Erik Beckjord** argued that Bigfoot is an "inter-dimensional shape-shifter that can warp in and out of physical reality." This is offered as an explanation for the persistent lack of physical evidence (no bodies, bones, or conclusive DNA) despite thousands of reported sightings.

**Inner Earth origin:** Some theorists (e.g., author **Kewaunee Lapseritis** in *The Sasquatch People and Their Interdimensional Connection*) claim Sasquatch are highly intelligent beings who live primarily underground or in other dimensions, emerging only occasionally to the surface. This view connects Bigfoot to broader inner Earth / hollow Earth narratives.

**UFO connection:** Researcher **Jack Cary** has noted that roughly 20% of Bigfoot sightings coincide with UFO events in the same area, leading some to hypothesize a connection -- either that Bigfoot are themselves alien beings, or that they are being studied/transported by UFOs.

**Environmental triggers:** Some proponents speculate that electromagnetic anomalies, geological hotspots (ley lines, quartz-rich formations), or solar activity could "thin" dimensional boundaries, enabling crossover events.

### Scientific assessment

There is no physical evidence (bones, scat, verified DNA, clear photographs) for the existence of Sasquatch, let alone for interdimensional or subterranean origins. The Patterson-Gimlin film (1967) remains the most cited visual evidence and continues to be debated, though most scientists consider it likely a hoax.

## 16. Raptors -- Alleged Reptilian Species Underground

### The reptilian conspiracy theory

The modern reptilian conspiracy theory was primarily popularized by British author **David Icke** in his 1999 book *The Biggest Secret*. Icke claims that tall, blood-drinking, shape-shifting **reptilian humanoids** from the **Alpha Draconis** star system have infiltrated human society by assuming human form, and now control world governments, banking systems, and media. They allegedly maintain underground bases.

The broader category of "reptilians" in conspiracy lore includes several alleged sub-types:
- **Draconians** (Dracos) -- tall, winged reptilian overlords
- **Reptoids** -- humanoid reptilians that can shapeshift
- **Raptors** -- sometimes described as a smaller, more aggressive reptilian species dwelling underground, occasionally associated with the "raptor" dinosaur body plan

### The Lacerta Files (1999)

In 1999, an anonymous Swedish researcher published the "**Lacerta Files**" -- purported transcripts of interviews with a reptilian being named "Lacerta." According to these texts, Lacerta's species evolved naturally on Earth millions of years before humans and now lives in underground cave systems. They allegedly use a form of mind control to appear human when walking on the surface.

The Lacerta Files claim that:
- Reptilians evolved from dinosaur-era ancestors on Earth
- Alien visitors genetically engineered humanity's ancestors
- Reptilians now live in vast underground civilizations
- They can telepathically project a human appearance

The anonymous origin, lack of any physical evidence, and the absence of corroborating testimony render the Lacerta Files unverifiable. Skeptic publication *The Skeptic* has described them as an example of "the persistence of appealing myths."

### Cultural and psychological context

A 2013 Public Policy Polling survey found that **4% of registered American voters** said they believed "lizard people" control our societies. Scholars have noted parallels between reptilian conspiracy theories and older antisemitic tropes about secret ruling cabals, a connection Icke has been criticized for.
