## 1. Ancient Builder Race

**Context on the map:** "Ruins of the Ancient Builder Race civilization lay scattered across the solar system" / "Built advanced transparent aluminum structures across 52 local star systems" / "Technologically & Spiritually Superior -- 6th Density"

### What adherents claim

The Ancient Builder Race (ABR) is a central concept in the Secret Space Program (SSP) narrative promoted by Corey Goode and amplified by David Wilcock. According to Goode, the SSP discovered a "technological wonderland" estimated to be over a billion years old, left behind by this race. The ABR allegedly left crystalline domes, pyramids, obelisks, and underground cities throughout our solar system and neighboring star systems. The structures are said to be made of a transparent aluminum-like material and protected by an energetic barrier erected around a 52-star local cluster roughly 500,000 years ago.

Goode claims the SSP discovered ABR technology on the Moon, Mars, asteroids, and even on the interstellar object Oumuamua when they allegedly boarded it in 2017-2018. According to the Anshar (alleged Inner Earth beings), billions of years ago this race was known as "the Guardians" before they "left our frequency."

### Skeptical perspective

There is zero physical evidence for any of these claims. Corey Goode has provided no verifiable documentation from any space program. The narrative is built entirely on his personal testimony and has never been independently corroborated by any scientific instrument, photograph, or credible whistleblower.

The Law of One (also called the Ra Material) is a series of 106 channeled sessions conducted between January 1981 and January 1984 by three members of L/L Research:

## 5. Venus -- Alleged Ancient Civilization, "H Buildings"

### What adherents claim

In the Law of One material, Ra claims Venus as its home world, stating that the Ra social memory complex evolved there approximately 2.6 billion years ago before ascending to higher densities. The Great Awakening Map labels Venus as the origin point of the Ancient Builder Race.

The "H Buildings" reference on the map refers to alleged miles-high tower structures on Venus, described as having an "H" shape when viewed from above or in profile. In the late 1980s, Soviet scientist Nikolai Ribinchikov reportedly claimed that Venera-13 probe data revealed roughly 20,000 geometrically regular formations resembling pyramids on the Venusian surface. Venus's surface temperature exceeds 450 degrees C (roughly 860 degrees F) with crushing atmospheric pressure (~90 Earth atmospheres) and sulfuric acid clouds. The European Space Agency has noted that Venus may once have had liquid water oceans and potentially habitable conditions early in its history, but there is no scientific evidence of any past civilization. Geometric features in radar images are attributed to volcanic geology and imaging artifacts.

## 6. Cydonia -- Face on Mars, Pyramids, 19.5 Degrees

**Context on the map:** "Cydonia" / "Pyramids -- 19.5 degrees" / "FACE on MARS -- Pharaoh Sarcophagus"

### The original discovery

Cydonia is a region in the northern hemisphere of Mars, imaged by the Viking 1 orbiter on July 25, 1976. One photograph (frame 35A72) showed a mesa that, under specific lighting conditions and at Viking's resolution (~50 meters/pixel), resembled a humanoid face approximately 1.5 km across. The image was released by NASA as a curiosity, but it ignited decades of speculation.

### Richard Hoagland's theories

Richard C. Hoagland, a former NASA consultant and science journalist, became the most prominent advocate for the artificial-origin hypothesis. Key claims:

- **The Face** is a constructed monument, possibly a sphinx-like structure
- **The D&M Pyramid** (named after discoverers Vincent DiPietro and Gregory Molenaar) is a five-sided pyramidal structure near the Face, exhibiting specific mathematical ratios including pi, e (Euler's number), and the square root of 2
- **The 19.5-degree latitude** is the critical "hyperdimensional" angle. When a tetrahedron is inscribed inside a sphere with one vertex at a pole, the other three vertices touch the sphere at 19.5 degrees latitude. Hoagland argued that major energy upwelling features on planets (Jupiter's Great Red Spot, Hawaii on Earth, Olympus Mons on Mars) cluster at this latitude, suggesting a universal "hyperdimensional physics"
- These relationships were published in Hoagland's book *The Monuments of Mars* (1987, updated editions through 2002) and detailed at his Enterprise Mission website

### What NASA's higher-resolution images show

When the Mars Global Surveyor (1998) and Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter (2007) reimaged the Face at resolutions as fine as ~0.25 meters/pixel, the face-like appearance disappeared. The feature is a natural mesa consistent with erosion patterns common in the Cydonia region. The D&M Pyramid likewise appears to be a natural hill when viewed at higher resolution and different sun angles.

The 19.5-degree correlation is considered by mathematicians and physicists to be a case of cherry-picking: the cited features do not actually cluster tightly at 19.47 degrees, and many equally prominent planetary features occur at other latitudes.

## 8. Mantids -- Praying Mantis Aliens in UFO Lore

**Context on the map:** "MANTIDS" (listed under Mars)

### What experiencers report

Mantis beings (also called Mantids or Insectoids) are among the most consistently described non-Grey alien types in UFO abduction literature. Key characteristics reported across decades:

- **Appearance:** 7-9 feet tall, extremely thin and elongated, segmented exoskeleton with an oily or reflective sheen, triangular head with large dark eyes, vestigial mandible-like mouth structures
- **Communication:** Telepathic, with clicking sounds and high-frequency tones used among themselves
- **Role in abductions:** Often described as supervisors or overseers directing Grey aliens during medical/genetic procedures. Many abductees describe them as being "in charge" during encounters

**Notable cases:**
- **Linda Porter (1963):** Under hypnosis, described being taken aboard a craft and encountering a "praying mantis about eight feet tall" in a room where she witnessed experiments involving human bodies in cylinders
- **Cincinnati nurse (1957):** Reported encountering a 3-foot-tall mantis-like entity two days after a V-shaped UFO sighting, documented by abduction researcher Brian Thompson
- **DMT experiences:** Mantis-like entities are frequently reported in N,N-dimethyltryptamine experiences, creating a crossover between psychedelic and UFO communities

In Goode's SSP narrative, Mantids are listed as one of the species encountered on Mars, living in underground areas. They appear on the Great Awakening Map specifically in the Mars section.

### Skeptical perspective

Sleep paralysis, false memory syndrome, suggestibility under hypnosis, and the cultural feedback loop of abduction narratives are the mainstream explanations. The consistency of descriptions may reflect shared cultural imagery rather than real encounters. The overlap with DMT entities suggests neurological rather than extraterrestrial origins.

## 9. Oumuamua -- Actual Science + Conspiracy Interpretations

**Context on the map:** "ANCIENT BUILDER RACE DERELICT SHIP 1B/YRS OLD" / "Enters through Outer Energetic Barrier and intercepted by SSP Alliance (2018)"

### The actual science

**1I/'Oumuamua** (Hawaiian for "scout" or "first distant messenger") was discovered on October 19, 2017, by the Pan-STARRS1 telescope at Haleakala Observatory, Hawaii. It was the first confirmed interstellar object to pass through our solar system.

**Observed properties:**
- Highly elongated shape, estimated 100-400 meters long with a roughly 6:1 length-to-width ratio
- No visible coma or outgassing tail (unlike a typical comet)
- Displayed non-gravitational acceleration -- it was speeding up as it moved away from the Sun in a way that could not be explained by gravity alone
- Reddish color consistent with organic-rich surface material
- Tumbling rotation with a period of about 8 hours

**The scientific debate:**

- **Hydrogen outgassing hypothesis (2023 consensus):** A study led by Jennifer Bergner (UC Berkeley) and Darryl Seligman (Cornell), published in *Nature*, proposed that cosmic ray bombardment during 'Oumuamua's interstellar journey converted water ice into trapped molecular hydrogen. As the object warmed near the Sun, this hydrogen was released, providing a gentle push without a visible coma. This is now the leading scientific explanation.
- **Avi Loeb's alien technology hypothesis:** Harvard astrophysicist Avi Loeb argued that 'Oumuamua could be a "light sail" -- a thin, flat piece of alien technology propelled by solar radiation pressure. He published this idea in a 2018 paper and expanded it in his 2021 book *Extraterrestrial*. His hypothesis requires an extremely thin, flat geometry (less than a millimeter thick). Most astronomers have criticized this as unnecessary speculation when natural explanations suffice.

### The conspiracy interpretation

On the Great Awakening Map, 'Oumuamua is described as a billion-year-old derelict ship of the Ancient Builder Race that was intercepted by the SSP Alliance when it entered through the "Outer Energetic Barrier." Goode claims that SSP personnel landed on and entered 'Oumuamua, discovering intact Ancient Builder Race technology inside.

## 10. Iapetus -- "Ancient Death Star" Theory

**Context on the map:** "Iapetus -- Ancient Death Star"

### The real moon

Iapetus is the third-largest moon of Saturn, with a diameter of about 1,470 km (roughly 914 miles). It was discovered by Giovanni Cassini in 1671 and has several genuinely unusual features:

- **Two-tone coloring:** One hemisphere is extremely dark (albedo ~0.03-0.05, as dark as coal) while the other is brilliantly bright (albedo ~0.5-0.6), giving it a yin-yang appearance
- **Equatorial ridge:** A massive mountain chain running along much of its equator, up to 20 km high, 20 km wide, and over 1,300 km long. It is one of the most striking geological features in the solar system and its origin remains debated (hypotheses include ring collapse, tectonic compression, and convective upwelling)
- **Walnut shape:** The equatorial ridge gives the moon a somewhat angular, walnut-like profile rather than a smooth sphere
- **Large impact basin:** A crater roughly 580 km across on one side

### The conspiracy theory

In 2005, Richard C. Hoagland published an analysis arguing that Iapetus is partially or fully artificially constructed. His arguments:

- The angular shape is unusual for a body of Iapetus's size, which should be roughly spherical under its own gravity
- The equatorial ridge is "too perfect" and resembles a weld seam where two hemispheres were joined
- Certain surface features, when contrast-enhanced, allegedly show geometric patterns
- He explicitly compared it to the Death Star from *Star Wars*, noting that the large crater resembles the superlaser dish

The Great Awakening Map simply labels it "Ancient Death Star," placing it alongside other alleged Ancient Builder Race artifacts in the Saturn system.

### What planetary scientists say

The equatorial ridge is genuinely mysterious and actively studied. However, no planetary scientist has proposed an artificial origin. Iapetus's density (~1.08 g/cm3) is consistent with a body made mostly of water ice with some rock, not the hollow metallic structure an artificial "Death Star" would require. The dark/light coloring is explained by thermal migration of water ice combined with swept-up dust from Saturn's Phoebe ring.

## 11. Ringmakers of Saturn -- Norman Bergrun

**Context on the map:** "RINGMAKERS OF SATURN -- 'B' Rings Reveal Ruins for 70ft. -- Norman Bergrun"

### The book and its author

Dr. Norman R. Bergrun (1920-2018) was a legitimate aerospace engineer who worked at NACA's Ames Research Laboratory for 12 years (NACA was the predecessor of NASA) and later at Lockheed Missiles and Space Company. He held a BSE from Cornell and an LLB (law degree) from LaSalle University Extension. He authored a NASA technical report on thermal ice prevention for aircraft.

In 1986, Bergrun self-published *Ringmakers of Saturn* through The Pentland Press in Edinburgh, Scotland. The book analyzes photographs taken by the Voyager 1 (1980) and Voyager 2 (1981) spacecraft of Saturn's rings.

### Key claims

- Bergrun identified what he called "Electromagnetic Vehicles" (EMVs) -- enormous cylindrical or cigar-shaped objects orbiting within Saturn's rings
- He estimated these objects to be up to 7,000 miles long (and in later interviews suggested some were 3-4 times the diameter of Earth)
- He proposed that these vehicles are actively constructing and maintaining Saturn's rings, hence the title "Ringmakers"
- He claimed that the Cassini Division and Enke Gap (gaps in the rings) are explained by EMV activity
- He connected these alleged craft to the Mare Orientale crater on Earth's Moon and the 1908 Tunguska explosion in Siberia

### Scientific assessment

Mainstream planetary science explains Saturn's ring structure through well-understood gravitational dynamics: shepherd moons, orbital resonances with Saturn's larger moons, and collisional physics among ring particles. The "objects" Bergrun identified in Voyager photographs are widely considered to be imaging artifacts, ring-edge effects, shadow phenomena, and overexposed areas on photographic plates. No subsequent mission (including Cassini, which orbited Saturn from 2004-2017 with vastly superior instruments) has detected any anomalous objects in the rings.

## 13. Maldek -- Exploded Planet Theory (Asteroid Belt)

**Context on the map:** "Maldek (Super Earth Exploded)" / "WAR WITH MARS" / "500K/yrs ago -- Maldek + Mars Ancient War"

### Historical roots of the hypothesis

The idea that the asteroid belt is the remains of a destroyed planet has a legitimate (if now discredited) scientific history:

- **Titius-Bode Law (1766/1772):** Johann Titius and Johann Bode noted a numerical pattern in planetary distances from the Sun that predicted a planet between Mars and Jupiter
- **Discovery of Ceres (1801):** Giuseppe Piazzi discovered Ceres in the predicted gap, initially thought to be the missing planet
- **Asteroid belt discovery:** As more objects were found in the same region, Heinrich Olbers proposed in 1802 that they were fragments of a destroyed planet he called "Phaeton"
- **Modern rejection:** Planetary scientists now agree the asteroid belt is *not* a destroyed planet. The total mass of the belt is less than 4% of Earth's Moon, far too little for a planet. Jupiter's gravitational influence prevented a planet from forming in this region during the solar system's accretion phase

The Great Awakening Map expands on this, adding that Maldek was a "Super Earth" that fought a devastating war with Mars before being destroyed. This war also allegedly devastated Mars's surface and atmosphere, forcing Martian survivors underground.

## 14. Torsion Field / Torsion Drives

**Context on the map:** "Torsion 'Warp' Drives"

### Scientific background -- Einstein-Cartan torsion

In legitimate physics, torsion refers to the antisymmetric part of the affine connection in Einstein-Cartan theory (ECT), a 1920s extension of general relativity developed by Elie Cartan. In ECT, the intrinsic spin of matter can produce a "torsion" of spacetime in addition to the curvature produced by mass-energy. This is real mathematical physics, but its effects are predicted to be significant only at extremely high densities (inside black holes or in the very early universe) and are unmeasurable with current technology.

### The pseudoscientific version

Beginning in the late 1980s in the Soviet Union, physicists Anatoly Akimov and Gennady Shipov led a government-funded research program claiming that:

- Torsion fields are generated by the spin of particles and can propagate through vacuum at speeds far exceeding light
- These fields can carry information and interact with consciousness
- Torsion field generators could be built for communication, healing, and propulsion

In 1991, the Russian Academy of Sciences, led by physicist Yevgeny Aleksandrov, exposed the program as fraudulent and an embezzlement of government research funding. The program was shut down.

Despite this, the concept persisted in fringe circles. Claims attributed to torsion fields include:

- **Propulsion:** A "torsion drive" could warp spacetime for faster-than-light travel. In 2008, a device branded as a "torsion field engine" was installed on the Russian satellite Yubileiny, but post-flight analysis by mainstream Russian scientists showed it had zero measurable effect on the satellite's orbit
- **Consciousness interaction:** Torsion fields are invoked as the mechanism by which consciousness affects matter (linking to remote viewing, psychokinesis, etc.)
- **Energy generation:** Claimed to enable overunity (free energy) devices

On the Great Awakening Map, "Torsion Warp Drives" are listed as the propulsion technology used by SSP craft, alongside zero-point energy systems.

### Scientific consensus

Torsion fields as described by Akimov and Shipov are classified as pseudoscience by the physics community. The theoretical Einstein-Cartan torsion is real but has nothing to do with the claims made in fringe literature. No torsion-based propulsion or communication device has ever been demonstrated to work.
